What is the goal?

Tap once to start and tap again when you believe exactly one second has passed. Lower deviation is better.

Five-lap runs are fast, the timing graph reveals your pattern, and the Daily Run gives you a clean reason to come back tomorrow.

Does it require an account?

No. Personal bests and streaks stay local. The game is designed to open fast and stay simple.
